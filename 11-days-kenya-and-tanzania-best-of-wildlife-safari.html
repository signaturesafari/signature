11 Days Kenya and Tanzania Best Wildlife Safari

Embark on an incredible 11-day journey that takes you through breathtaking landscapes and abundant wildlife. Day 2: Nairobi-Masai Mara National Reserve

Depart early in the morning for the Maasai Mara National Reserve, one of the last places on Earth where wildlife thrives in numbers reminiscent of the historic plains and forests of Africa. As the northern extension of the expansive Serengeti ecosystem, the Mara is famed for the spectacular seasonal migration of thousands of wildebeest, zebras, and their predators. Arrive in time for lunch, followed by an afternoon game drive.

Destination: Masai Mara National Reserve

Accommodation: Zebra Plains Mara Camp

Meal plan: Full Board (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

Day 3: Masai Mara National Reserve

After breakfast, we embark on a full-day game drive with a packed lunch provided. The reserve is renowned for its black-maned lions and abundant resident wildlife, and it's one of the few places where you can spot the Big Five during a morning game drive. Many cheetahs in the reserve are so accustomed to vehicles that they often seek shade beneath them and occasionally climb onto the roofs for a better view of potential prey. Bird enthusiasts will be thrilled to know that almost 500 species have been recorded here, including 16 species of eagles, numerous hawks and falcons, 6 species of vultures, 8 species of storks, 4 species of bustards (including the Kori bustard, the world's heaviest flying bird), and 9 species of sunbirds.

Destination: Masai Mara National Reserve

Accommodation: Zebra Plains Mara Camp

Meal plan: Full Board (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

Day 4: Masai Mara - Serengeti National Park

After breakfast, you'll proceed to the Mara Airstrip for your flight to Tanzania's Serengeti National Park. Upon arrival, you'll be greeted by your driver and embark on an afternoon safari in this famed park. The Serengeti is renowned for its large populations of wildebeest, zebras, and antelopes, providing an excellent opportunity to see predators like lions, leopards, and cheetahs, as well as herbivores such as elephants, hippos, and giraffes.

Destination: Serengeti National Park

Accommodation: Embalakai Authentic Camps

Meal plan: Full Board (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

Day 5: Serengeti National Park (Kogatende)

After your morning meal, you'll commence your safari in the Kogatende area, exploring the rocky outcrops (kopjes) while on the lookout for carnivores and the large groups of the Wildebeest Migration that are preparing to cross the Mara River into Kenya. The Mara River, home to significant populations of hippos and Nile crocodiles, serves as the final major obstacle before the wildebeests and zebras can reach the relative safety of the Masai Mara. If you're patient and a bit fortunate, you might even have the chance to witness the thrilling river crossing, where wildebeests and zebras brave the river's currents and crocodile presence in their quest for safety on the opposite bank. Following your adventurous day, you'll enjoy dinner and spend the night in the heart of the Serengeti National Park, based on your chosen accommodation option and standard.

Destination: North Serengeti (Kogatende)

Accommodation: Mara Heritage Camp

Meal plan: Full Board (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

Day 10: Arusha - Amboseli National Park

After breakfast, you'll depart from Arusha and begin your journey back to Kenya. The drive offers picturesque views, and you'll go through immigration procedures before meeting your new driver and continuing to the park. Amboseli National Park, known as the "Royal Court of Kilimanjaro," awaits you. Enjoy an afternoon safari drive, where you'll encounter herds of wildebeests and zebras, majestic lions, stealthy hyenas, various antelope species, and some of Kenya's largest elephant herds, all set against the breathtaking backdrop of Mount Kilimanjaro.
