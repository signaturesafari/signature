6 Days Perfect Botswana Family Safari

Description

6 Days Perfect Botswana Family Safari

Begin a six-day journey through Botswana, commencing with three days at Boteti Safari Lodge for immersive full-day safaris or tranquil mokoro excursions. Transition to Leopard Plains for two days of thrilling wildlife encounters, highlighted by sunset drives and safaris in Makgadikgadi Park. Wrap up with a captivating morning excursion before heading back to Maun, relishing indelible memories amidst Botswana's breathtaking scenery and rich wildlife.

Day 1: Arrival and Welcome to Boteti Safari Lodge

Upon your arrival in Maun, our hospitable staff will extend a warm welcome and escort you to the captivating Boteti Safari Lodge. Upon settling into your cozy accommodations, you'll be treated to refreshing drinks and heartfelt smiles. Take a moment to unwind and embrace the tranquil ambiance of the lodge. In the evening, delight in a delectable dinner showcasing the flavors of local cuisine, setting the stage for a peaceful night's rest, ensuring you're rejuvenated for the thrilling adventures awaiting you.

Destination: Okavango Delta

Accommodation: Boteti Tented Safari Lodge

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 2: Exploring Moremi or Mokoro Safari

Start your day with the serene melodies of the African wilderness and savor a nourishing breakfast to energize your adventures. Today, you have two enticing options: embark on an exciting full-day safari in the renowned Moremi Game Reserve, where you'll encounter diverse wildlife amidst breathtaking landscapes. Alternatively, immerse yourself in the peace of the Okavango Delta with a full-day mokoro safari, silently gliding through its waterways in traditional dugout canoes. After a thrilling day of discovery, return to the lodge for a sumptuous dinner and relish in sharing tales of your safari encounters beneath the glittering canopy of stars.

Destination: Okavango Delta

Accommodation: Boteti Tented Safari Lodge

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 3: Relaxed Safari Day and Sunset Mokoro Safari on the Boteti River

Begin your day with yet another delightful breakfast before indulging in a leisurely morning at the lodge. You might opt for a refreshing nature walk or simply unwind by the pool, soaking in the serene atmosphere. As the afternoon approaches, set off on a picturesque sunset mokoro safari along the Boteti River. Glide gracefully through the calm waters, basking in the mesmerizing sight of the sun dipping below the horizon, casting a warm, enchanting glow over the surroundings. Conclude your day back at the lodge with a final dinner, relishing the rich flavors of Botswana before bidding the night farewell.

Destination: Okavango Delta

Accommodation: Boteti Tented Safari Lodge

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 4: Transfer to Leopard Plains and Sunset Drive

Following breakfast, say goodbye to Boteti Safari Lodge as you journey to Leopard Plains, situated near Makgadikgadi Park. Upon reaching your destination, take a moment to acclimate to your new environment before embarking on an afternoon sunset drive. Be sure to have your camera at the ready as you scan the horizon for wildlife amidst the expansive African plains. As dusk approaches and the sun paints the sky in hues of orange and pink, return to Leopard Plains for a delectable dinner and some much-needed relaxation.

Destination: Okavango Delta

Accommodation: Boteti Tented Safari Lodge

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 5: Explore the Incredible Makgadikgadi National Park

Today, get ready for an exhilarating safari adventure to Makgadikgadi Park and its vast salt pans. Delve into this extraordinary landscape, which hosts a diverse array of wildlife specially adapted to the challenging desert environment. Keep a keen lookout for meerkats, ostriches, and maybe even catch a glimpse of the elusive brown hyena as you navigate through the captivating terrain. Pause for a scenic picnic lunch amidst the awe-inspiring scenery, rejuvenating yourself before delving further into the exploration of this remarkable wilderness.

Destination: Makgadikgadi Pans National Park

Accommodation: Leopard Plains

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 6: Morning Excursion and Return to Maun

On your last morning at Leopard Plains, take your time to relish a leisurely breakfast before setting out on a morning adventure. Choose between a guided nature walk or a visit to nearby cultural sites to enrich your experience. Afterward, bid adieu to Makgadikgadi as you make your way back to Maun, reminiscing on the remarkable experiences and treasured moments forged during your Botswana safari escapade.

Meal plan: Breakfast & lunch
