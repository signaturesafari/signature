5 Days Chobe river and Okavango delta Safari

Experience the best of Botswana's wildlife havens with our 5-day Chobe and Delta safari, seamlessly blending the mesmerizing landscapes of the Chobe River region and the Okavango Delta. Offering unparalleled game viewing opportunities and a striking contrast between the two locales, this safari promises a truly enriching adventure. Immerse yourself in the splendor of these regions from intimate tented camps, where you'll uncover the magic of morning, afternoon, and nocturnal game drives, tranquil river cruises, serene mokoro excursions, and immersive local village tours. For those seeking further exploration, this journey seamlessly integrates with extensions to Victoria Falls or captivating Cape Town, ensuring a comprehensive African experience.

Day 1-2: Chobe River

Today, you'll embark on a transfer to the Chobe River, where you'll spend 2 nights indulging in the comforts of Chobe Elephant Camp on a full-board basis. Following breakfast, enjoy a scenic 2-hour transfer to Chobe National Park, where your next retreat, Chobe Elephant Camp, awaits. Nestled atop a forested ridge, this traditional safari camp offers breathtaking river vistas from every angle. Experience the essence of Chobe through game drives along the river floodplains and leisurely boat cruises, ensuring a comprehensive exploration of this remarkable landscape. The dry season, spanning from May to October, showcases the river at its peak, attracting an abundance of wildlife, notably elephants congregating along the water's edge during hot afternoons. With the rich game densities come predators, particularly lions and increasingly sighted leopards, stealthily navigating the riverine forest. Delve deeper into the heart of Chobe during river cruises, where encounters with crocodiles, hippos, giant monitor lizards, and majestic African Fish-eagles await at every turn.

Destination: Chobe River

Accommodation: Chobe Elephant Camp

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 3-4: A Pearl in the Okavango Delta

Today, you'll embark on a shared charter flight to Kadizora Camp nestled in the heart of the Okavango Delta, where you'll relish in a two-night stay on a full-board basis. Departing from the Khwai Reserve, you'll soar by shared charter towards the northern reaches of the Okavango Delta. Your ultimate destination, Kadizora Camp, awaits amidst a serene setting beneath the shade of trees, offering sweeping views of lush floodplains adorned with grazing antelope. Your tented chalet awaits, boasting a cozy lounge, well-appointed en suite bathroom, and a private viewing deck for moments of tranquil contemplation. Kadizora Camp, typical of delta accommodations, presents an array of thrilling activities including 4X4 game drives, captivating boating excursions, immersive walking safaris, and picturesque canoe safaris. For an extraordinary experience, guests visiting between April and September can opt for a hot air balloon flight, offering a bird's-eye perspective of the Delta's rich tapestry of wildlife. With its abundant wildlife and diverse landscapes, Kadizora Camp promises to elevate your safari experience to new heights.

Destination: Okavango Delta

Accommodation: Kadizora Camp

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 4: Transfer to Leopard Plains and Sunset Drive

Following breakfast, say goodbye to Boteti Safari Lodge as you journey to Leopard Plains, situated near Makgadikgadi Park. Upon reaching your destination, take a moment to acclimate to your new environment before embarking on an afternoon sunset drive. Be sure to have your camera at the ready as you scan the horizon for wildlife amidst the expansive African plains. As dusk approaches and the sun paints the sky in hues of orange and pink, return to Leopard Plains for a delectable dinner and some much-needed relaxation.

Destination: Okavango Delta

Accommodation: Boteti Tented Safari Lodge

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 5: Explore the Incredible Makgadikgadi National Park

Today, get ready for an exhilarating safari adventure to Makgadikgadi Park and its vast salt pans. Delve into this extraordinary landscape, which hosts a diverse array of wildlife specially adapted to the challenging desert environment. Keep a keen lookout for meerkats, ostriches, and maybe even catch a glimpse of the elusive brown hyena as you navigate through the captivating terrain. Pause for a scenic picnic lunch amidst the awe-inspiring scenery, rejuvenating yourself before delving further into the exploration of this remarkable wilderness.

Destination: Makgadikgadi Pans National Park

Accommodation: Leopard Plains

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 6: Morning Excursion and Return to Maun

On your last morning at Leopard Plains, take your time to relish a leisurely breakfast before setting out on a morning adventure. Choose between a guided nature walk or a visit to nearby cultural sites to enrich your experience. Afterward, bid adieu to Makgadikgadi as you make your way back to Maun, reminiscing on the remarkable experiences and treasured moments forged during your Botswana safari escapade.

Meal plan: Breakfast & lunch
