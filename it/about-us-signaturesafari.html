About Signature Safari

Leading Safari & Mt Kilimanjaro Climbing Experts

Signature Safari and Tours, founded in 2006, is a Tanzanian-owned company specializing in bespoke adventure experiences, including mountain summits and safaris. With over twelve years of expertise, their team of passionate mountain and safari guides focuses on delivering exceptional service and authentic experiences. They offer pre-designed itineraries and customizable packages that include cultural encounters with local tribes and artisans. Committed to client satisfaction, Signature Safari ensures meticulous attention to detail from the first contact to the farewell at the airport, aiming to showcase the friendliness and spirit of Tanzania.

How Signature Safari was Founded?

Mr. Innocent Villone Kawiche, CEO and co-founder of Signature Safari Tanzania, was born and raised in the Kilimanjaro Region. In 2006, he co-founded Signature Safari Tanzania, drawing on his extensive experience leading adventures up Mount Kilimanjaro and wildlife safaris across East Africa. Joined by his relatives, Alice and Edwin Kawiche, the family leveraged their combined expertise to establish a family-run company. The company is supported by a board of advisors and a global network of affiliates, ensuring quality decision-making and direction. Signature Safari has led various groups, including famous individuals like Mr. Peter Lagarias, co-founder of Rotaplast, and an Indian family with a 14-year-old girl who summited Mount Kilimanjaro in July 2022.
