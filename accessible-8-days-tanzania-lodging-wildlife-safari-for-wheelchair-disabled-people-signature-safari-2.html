Day 1: Arrival – Arusha

Upon your arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport, your professional driver from Signature Safari will greet you and transfer you to Arusha town for hotel check-in, relaxation, dinner, and an overnight stay. In case you arrive early in Arusha and have spare time, we can arrange a complimentary town tour for you. Additionally, if the sky is clear and weather conditions are favorable, you may also have a chance to see the magnificent Mount Kilimanjaro and Meru from far on this day.Tulia is the perfect oasis for guests looking for a private and peaceful retreat. From its Africanised architecture to its cosy and aesthetically pleasing interior, Tulia Hotel is a ‘highly individualized property’.

Accommodation: Tulia Boutique Hotel and Spa

DAY 2: TARANGIRE NATIONAL PARK

We’ll begin our day early in the morning, departing from Arusha town with a picnic lunch in tow, for a full day game drive in Tarangire National Park. The park is renowned for its majestic baobab trees that tower over the animals grazing beneath them, and the Tarangire River is a central feature of the park that’s famous for its large herds of elephants – some of the largest in Africa. During your visit, you may have the chance to see a variety of wildlife including lions, leopards, cheetahs, lesser kudu, buffaloes, oryx, eland, giraffes, and zebras. After the game drive, you’ll return to your accommodation to relax, and enjoy a delicious dinner and overnight stay.Dinner and Overnight will be at Chem Chem Safari Lodge.

Accommodation: Chem Chem Safari Lodge

DAY 3: TRANSIT TO SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK

After enjoying a delicious breakfast at our accommodation, we’ll set out towards the Serengeti National Park, traveling through vast stretches of grasslands. En route, we’ll have a game drive, before continuing on to the Serengeti. Widely considered to be one of the most impressive wildlife sanctuaries in the world, the Serengeti offers an unforgettable experience. In the late afternoon, we’ll embark on a game drive through the endless plains, providing an opportunity to see the great herds of wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles, as well as lions lounging in the shade. During the full-day game drive, we’ll also have a picnic lunch. The day ends with a satisfying dinner and a peaceful night’s sleep at your accommodation in Central Serengeti.

Accommodation: Serengeti Heritage Tented Camp

DAY 4: SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK

Undoubtedly, the Serengeti National Park is one of the world’s most spectacular wildlife sanctuaries. In the late afternoon, you’ll embark on a game drive in the vast plains of the Serengeti, with the opportunity to see enormous herds of wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles, as well as lions lounging in the shade. After an eventful day, you can enjoy a hearty dinner and a peaceful overnight stay at your accommodation in Central Serengeti.You will be taken to the accommodation at Serengeti Heritage Tented Camps.

Accommodation: Serengeti Heritage Tented Camp Northern

DAY 5: CENTRAL SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK

After enjoying a delicious and filling breakfast, embark on a game drive to explore the central portion of Serengeti National Park today. This area, known as the Seronera, is renowned for its diverse wildlife, including a significant population of lions and leopards. The Seronera River, which runs through this region, attracts many of the park’s species due to its reliable water source. Even during the off-season migration, there are many opportunities to spot lion prides and witness their interactions. A picnic lunch will be provided during the full-day game drive. We remain flexible with your preferences and can customize the day’s itinerary based on your interests. With the vastness of the Serengeti, we can explore different areas and observe a variety of animal behaviors, including hunting and fighting. The day ends with a hearty dinner and a restful night’s sleep in your accommodation. You will be taken to the accommodation at Serengeti Heritage Tented Camps.

Accommodation: Serengeti Heritage Tented Camp

DAY 6: SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK - NGORONGORO

After a hearty breakfast, you’ll set out on a game drive to explore the central region of the Serengeti National Park. The Serengeti is famous for its abundant population of lions and leopards, and the Seronera area in the central region is home to some of the park’s most diverse and thriving wildlife habitats. The Seronera River attracts a variety of animals, making it an ideal location for game viewing. Even during the off season of the migration, this area is home to many lion prides, making it an exciting place to spot wildlife all year round.After lunch at the accommodation, you’ll pack up and begin your journey to Ngorongoro, with an en-route game drive or transfer. You’ll have a delicious dinner and a comfortable night’s sleep at your new accommodation, getting some rest for the exciting day ahead. The following day, you’ll explore the Ngorongoro Conservation Area and take in the breathtaking scenery before returning to your accommodation for dinner and overnight.Ngorongoro Rhino Lodge, situated only a few hundred meters from the crater’s rim, offers a perfect base to explore the Ngorongoro Crater and its surroundings.

Accommodation: Ngorongoro Rhino Lodge

DAY 7: NGORONGORO CRATER– (NGORONGORO CONSERVATION AREA)

After an early breakfast, you’ll depart for a game drive at Ngorongoro Crater, descending 600 meters into the world’s largest inactive volcanic caldera. This magnificent natural wonder has a floor of around 260 square kilometers and a depth of over 2000 feet. It’s surrounded by a ring of extinct volcanoes and dotted with watering holes that attract a wide range of wildlife, including 30,000 animals such as African elephants, buffalos, black rhinos, hippos, hyenas, cheetahs and lions. The compact nature of the crater means that you can see a vast variety of animals and birds in a relatively short amount of time, and there’s always plenty of animal action to capture on camera during the 5-6 hour game drive. After the tour, you’ll be taken to Chem Chem Safari Lodge for dinner and overnight, full of beautiful scenery and memories.

Accommodation: Chem Chem Safari Lodge

DAY 8: LAKE MANYARA NATIONAL PARK

Our driver guide will be waiting for you at your accommodation after breakfast to kick off your safari to Lake Manyara National Park. Despite being one of Tanzania’s smallest national parks, it boasts the most dramatic wildlife area in the country. The park is dominated by a huge shallow salt lake that covers about two-thirds of the park and lies at the base of the Great Rift Valley. Due to its rich and varied habitats, the park is home to different animal species such as herds of zebra, giraffe, warthog, wildebeest, buffalo, African elephant, and the famous Lake Manyara tree-climbing lions. Countless numbers of flamingos attracted by green algae around the Lake can also be seen. This compact park allows you to see many animal and bird species, as well as trees and the beautiful Lake of Manyara. Afternoon, you will be taken to your accommodation in Arusha.

Accommodation: Tulia Boutique Hotel and Spa

DEPARTURE DAY

Airport drop off depend on the flight schedule. You will be picked up from you hotel to the Kilimanjaro International Airport ready to catch your flight to your next destination.
