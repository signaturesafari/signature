4 Days Luxurious Uganda Safari Highlight

4 Days Uganda valley view tour will take you to Kidepo Valley National Park that is located in the remote far northeastern corner of Uganda, nested between Napore and Zulia mountain ranges. This park is the only arid savannah national park in Uganda and it supports a wider variety of mammal species than any other protected area in Uganda.

Day 1: Arrival to Kampala

Upon arrival at Kampala International Airport, you will be welcomed by your driver guide and after a brief briefing, you will be transferred to the hotel for dinner and overnight.

Day 2: Kampala-Kidepo valley National Park

Leave Kampala for Kidepo Valley National Park by air relax at the rest camp till lunch. After lunch, proceed with afternoon game drives in the park The park is a home to elephant, zebra, giraffe cheetah, lesser kudu wild dog and ostrich. Dinner and overnight at the camp.

Day 3: Kidepo Valley-Kananorok hot springs

After breakfast, spend the entire day excursion to the Kananorok hot springs an incredible sport. Evening ?Sundowner? drink overlooking the Narus valley plains. You will have all meals and overnight in your Rest camp.

Day 4: Kidepo valley

Wake up at 0600hrs for early morning game drives in the park at 0900hrs return to the rest camp for breakfast. After breakfast, relax at leisure in the camp, at 1600hrs proceed with evening game viewing. You will have lunch, dinner and overnight in your rest camp.

Day 5: Kidepo Valley-Kampala

Breakfast followed by a short bush walk along the Narus river, then return to the camp, check out for Kampala.
