3 Days Mount Meru Climbing Highlights

A 3 days climb up Mount Meru is an excellent choice for trekkers looking to take their time to acclimate and enjoy the stunning scenery. Here's a detailed itinerary for a 5-day trek:

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival Day (JRO) - Pre-Climb Briefing

Your mountain guide or one of representative will meet you at Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO) and escort you to your accommodations in Arusha. The drive from the airport to your hotel is about 45min.

Pre-climbing Briefing

At a specified time, your guide will gather you for the pre- climbing briefing that includes sharing all the necessary details and check all of your personal mountain gears. Going through the gears is very important as we have to make sure you are set for the trek and if you are missing something, your guide will organize it with the renting shops.

Late Arrivals

If your flight arrivals late or you are too tired to receive the pre- climbing briefing, your guide will still be there to make sure your gears are checked and the pre- briefing would be rescheduled the next morning.

Note: Please note your accommodation for this night is not included in your package price but once you confirm your booking we will share different accommodation options and you will choose the option that works better for your budget.

Day 2: Trekking to Miriakamba Hut (2,516m/8,254ft)

After breakfast, your mountain crew will collect you from your hotel in Arusha and drive for about an hour to Momella Gate, where you will complete entry formalities. You will have hot lunch at the gate before you begin your climb. Today trek will be through the grassland then gradually into the rainforest where you will spot giraffe, zebra, colobus monkeys and great number of birdlife. You will arrive in Miriakamba in time for evening leisure, dinner and overnight.

Distance: 10km

Hiking Time: 4-6hrs

Habitat: Montane Forest

Accommodation: Miriakamba Hut (3 Meals)

Day 3: Trek through the rainforest to Saddle Hut (3,570m/ 11.712.6ft)

After a hearty breakfast, your ascent to Saddle Hut begins. Today's walk will be steeper than the earlier day but in a well-maintained path rolls up through the rainforest. Snacks will be served at elephant ridge with a great view of Meru Summit and Mt. Kilimanjaro on the other side. After a short rest, you continue to Saddle Hut (3570m), on a wide col amid the slopes of Meru and Little Meru. At Saddle Hut, you will have hot lunch followed by a short acclimatization trek to the summit of Little Meru (3,820m) that offers superb views of Meru's summit, the crater and at the top of the ash cone you will see Kilimanjaro towering in a distance. Later on, return to Saddle hut for early dinner ready for summit day!

Distance: 8km

Hiking Time: 3-5hrs

Accommodation: Saddle Hut (3 Meals)

Day 4: Summit Day! Sunrise ascent of Mount Meru (4,566m/14,980ft) down to Momella Gate

At 2am you will wake up for quick snacks before you start your final climb steeply to Rhino point (3800m) and furthermore to Cobra Point (4350m). We reach the summit (4,566m) on time for sunrise with unlimited possibilities of seeing Kilimanjaro Peak above the clouds. If the weather permits, you will also have spectacular view of African Plains from the top. The final part of the climb is along a spectacular narrow ridge line between the sheer inner cliffs and the sloping outer wall of the crater. As you descend, you will stop at Saddle Hut for brunch and a quick shower if you wish. Later on, descend further down to Miriakamba Hut for dinner and overnight.

Distance: 8km up, 13km down

Hiking Time: 10-12hrs

Habitat: Alpine Desert Moorland and Montane forest
