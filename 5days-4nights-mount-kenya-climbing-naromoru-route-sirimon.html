05 Days Mount Kenya Climbing

Description

05 Days Mount Kenya Climbing Highlights

Mount Kenya Climbing Sirimon Naromoru Route tour takes you to climb Mount Kenya; this is an African challenging mountain. Although good health, fitness and experience is required but the mountain team with professional and qualified guide and porters will help you to make it. You will enjoy the trekking it self, landscape and high altitude on your way thru various points of views as per itinerary.

Detailed Itinerary

  • Itinerary
  • Cost & Book
Day 1: Arrival to Nairobi

Upon arrival at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport in Nairobi, you will be welcomed by your driver guide and after a brief briefing, you will be transferred to Tamadind tree hotel for dinner and overnight. Ideally located just 5 km southwest of the City within the spacious Carnivore grounds and its iconic Carnivore Restaurant, Tamarind Tree Hotel has easy access to Nairobi, its national park, and the country’s diverse attractions via the neighboring Nairobi Wilson Airport and Southern Bypass.With the new expressway, you will only spend 15 Minutes from the Jomo Kenyatta International Airport to Tamarind Tree Hotel The nearby suburbs are among Nairobi’s liveliest and most popular with world-class shopping malls, and restaurants.

Accommodation: Tamarind Tree Hotel Nairobi

Day 2: Nairobi-Nanyuki (1950m)

In the morning at 0830hrs pick up from your hotel in Nairobi and transferred to Nanyuki for acclimatization, lunch, dinner and overnight at the lodge.

Meal Plan: Full meal

Accommodation: Sweetwaters Serena Lodge.

Day 3: Nanyuki-Old Moses (2600m) 3hrs

After breakfast, then the van will take you to the starting point of your climbing, at the Sirimon Park gate. Trek thru the rain forest, taking lunch en-route, proceed to old Moses hut for dinner and overnight.

Meal Plan: Full Board

Accommodation: Old Moses Camp

Day 4: Old Moses-Shiptons Camp (4200m)

Wake up in the /Midnight/at 0230hrs, Walk up to the summit (point lenanaAfter an early morning breakfast, walk thru Mackindas Valley with your lunch box, later arrive Shiptons camp for dinner and overnight.

Meal Plan: Full Board

Accommodation: Shiptons Camp

Day 5: Shiptons - Point lenana (4985m) Met station

In the Mid night, wake up at 0230 hrs; proceed with hike up to the summit, point lenana. After a joyful time on the peak, descend to the Mackinders hut (4300m) for breakfast. Take a packed lunch with you down to the Met station camp for dinner and overnight.

Meal Plan: Full Board

Elevation: 12,205 ft to 15,430 ft

Distance: 10 km/6 miles

Hiking Time: 6-8 hours

Habitat: Alpine Desert
