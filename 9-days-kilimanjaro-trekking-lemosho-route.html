9 Days Kilimanjaro Trekking Lemosho Route

This long Affordable Kilimanjaro Trek Lemosho little-used route parallels the lower part of the Shira Route. Signature safari leads treks up the Lemosho Route. The Lemosho Route is an unspoiled, remote, little-used, and beautiful way up to the Shira Plateau. Above the plateau, you approach Kilimanjaro’s great western Breach, then follow the south circuit to reach the easier Barafu Route. This is one of the few routes where an armed ranger accompanies our groups, since there are still animals in the forest around the Lemosho Glades.

09 Days Kilimanjaro Trekking Lemosho Route

Day 1: Arrival at the airport

After arrival at the airport (Kilimanjaro / Arusha), your private driver guide will pick you up at the airport and transfer you to your accommodation for dinner and overnight awaiting for your departure on the following day. Your safari guide will meet you at your accommodation for the briefing and equipment checkup.

Accomodation: Arusha Planet lodge

Meal plan: Bed & Breakfast

Day 2: Arusha to Lemosho Gate to Big Tree Camp

Hiking time: 3 hours, Distance: About 9 km's, Habitat: Rain Forest

After breakfast at your hotel, embark on a two-and-a-half-hour drive from Arusha (1400m) to Londorossi Park Gate (2150m). At the entrance gate, guides and porters distribute supplies and register with the Tanzania National Parks Authority (TANAPA). Begin your 3-hour hike into the rainforest, keeping an eye out for Colobus monkeys! Lunch will be provided at the gate before continuing through undisturbed forest to the first campsite. Porters, arriving early, set up tents and boil water, while the chef prepares snacks and dinner. Be prepared for freezing temperatures at night!

Day 3: Big Tree to Shira Camp 1 to Shira Camp 2

Hiking time: 18 hours, Distance: About 18 km's, Habitat: Moorland

Exit the forest and enter a savannah of tall grasses, heather, and volcanic rock draped with lichen. Ascend through rolling hills and cross streams to reach Shira Ridge before descending to Shira 1 camp. Enjoy the amazing view of Kibo from across the plateau as you walk east towards Kibo’s glaciered peak, reaching Shira 2 camp on moorland meadows by a stream.

Day 4: Shira Camp 2 to Lava Tower to Barranco Camp

Hiking time: 8 hours, Distance: About 10 km's, Habitat: Semi desert

Continue eastward from the Shira Plateau, passing the junction towards the peak of Kibo. Change direction to the southeast towards the Lava Tower, known as the “Shark’s Tooth.” Descend to Barranco camp, where you'll rest, enjoy dinner, and spend the night.

Day 5: Barranco Camp to Karanga Camp

Hiking time: 4 hours, Distance: About 7 km's, Habitat: Semi desert

After breakfast, face the famous Barranco Wall, possibly the steepest part of the route. No technical climbing is necessary, but some spots may require the use of hands for better grip. Pass the last water spot as you reach Karanga Camp. After a well-deserved meal, retire for the night.

Day 6: Karanga Camp to Barafu Camp

Hiking time: 3 hours, Distance: About 2 km's, Habitat: Alpine Desert

Trek to Barafu Camp through the dry landscape, feeling the ground crack beneath your boots. Excitement builds as the rocky Barafu Camp comes into view, offering a stunning view of Mawenzi Peak and the summit path. After an early dinner and last-minute preparations, rest for the night.

Day 7: Summit Attempt to Barafu Camp to Uhuru Peak to Mweka

Hiking time: 7 hours to reach Uhuru Peak, 7 hours to descend to Mweka, Distance: About 14 km's, Habitat: Stone scree and ice-capped summit

Wake up around 2300hrs for the climb to Uhuru Peak. Head northwest and ascend through heavy scree towards Stella Point on the crater rim. Stop at Stella Point for a short rest and enjoy the magnificent sunrise. Proceed to Uhuru Peak, where you may encounter snow. Descend to Barafu Camp for a brief rest before continuing to Mweka Hut. Dinner and washing water will be prepared as you descend into the forest.

Day 8: Mweka Camp to Mweka Gate

After an early breakfast, embark on a scenic 3-hour hike back to the Park gate. Sign your name and details at Mweka Gate, where successful climbers receive summit certificates. Celebrate with your mountain crew before being transferred to Arusha at your accommodation for dinner and overnight stay.

Accommodation: Arusha Planet lodge

Meal plan: Full Board (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

Day 9: Onward Travel

After breakfast or lunch, you will be transferred to the airport for your departure flight to the next destination.
