Tanzania Accommodation

Discover Tanzania Accommodation Options

When exploring the incredible landscapes and diverse wildlife of Tanzania, your choice of accommodation can greatly enhance your experience. Tanzania offers a range of accommodations to suit every traveler's needs, from luxury lodges and tented camps to budget-friendly options. Each option provides a unique way to immerse yourself in the stunning surroundings.

HIGH-END LUXURY ACCOMMODATION

Elewana Arusha Coffee Lodge

Nestled amidst the sprawling grounds of Tanzania’s largest coffee plantation, Elewana Arusha Coffee Lodge offers an oasis of calm and luxury just moments away from Arusha’s bustling city center. This exquisite lodge provides a perfect starting point for your Tanzanian safari adventure, blending elegant accommodations with the charm and history of a working coffee plantation.

Lemala Mpingo Ridge Lodge

Nestled on the picturesque ridges of the Tarangire National Park, Lemala Mpingo Ridge Lodge offers a sublime blend of luxury, comfort, and breathtaking natural beauty. Perfectly positioned to provide sweeping views of the Tarangire River and the park’s iconic baobab trees, this lodge promises an unforgettable safari experience.

The Manor at Ngorongoro

Nestled in the lush landscape of the Ngorongoro Conservation Area, The Manor at Ngorongoro offers an unparalleled blend of luxury, elegance, and African charm. This exquisite property is set within a 1,500-acre Arabica coffee estate, providing a serene and picturesque retreat for discerning travelers.

Melia Ngorongoro

Discover the unparalleled beauty and comfort of Melia Ngorongoro Lodge, an exquisite accommodation nestled on the edge of the world-renowned Ngorongoro Crater. Perfectly positioned to offer breathtaking views of the crater and its diverse wildlife, Melia Ngorongoro is an ideal choice for travelers seeking a luxurious and immersive safari experience.

Four Seasons Safari Lodge Serengeti

Nestled in the heart of one of the world’s most celebrated wildlife reserves, the Four Seasons Safari Lodge Serengeti offers an extraordinary blend of luxury and adventure. Set amidst the vast, untamed plains of the Serengeti National Park, this lodge is your gateway to an unforgettable safari experience.
