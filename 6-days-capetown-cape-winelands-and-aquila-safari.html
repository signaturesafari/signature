6 Days Cape Town, Cape-winelands and Aquila Safari

Description

6 Days Cape Town, Cape-winelands and Aquila Safari

Experience the pinnacle of our tour with a captivating city exploration of Cape Town, renowned as one of the world's most stunning cities. Venture on a mesmerizing Cape Peninsula tour and indulge in a full-day adventure through the exquisite Cape Winelands, where you'll savor some of the finest wines globally. Conclude your journey with a memorable overnight stay at Aquila Game Reserve, complete with both sunrise and sunset safaris for an immersive wildlife encounter.

Day 1: Arrival Cape Town

Upon arrival at Cape Town Airport, our dedicated representative will warmly greet you and facilitate your transfer to the hotel. Following check-in, take a moment to refresh before embarking on an enriching sightseeing tour. Explore iconic landmarks such as Bo-Kaap, the vibrant V&A Waterfront, historic Greenmarket Square, and the stately Houses of Parliament. Later, ascend Table Mountain via cable car and behold the breathtaking sunset, treating yourself to a panoramic vista of the Mother City. Conclude the day with a return to the hotel for a restful overnight stay.

Destination: Cape Town

Accommodation: The Capetonian

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 2: Cape Town City Orientation Tour & Table Mountain Tour

Indulge in a delicious breakfast at the hotel before embarking on the Cape Town City Orientation Tour. Your itinerary includes visits to Bo-Kaap, the bustling V&A Waterfront, historic Greenmarket Square, and the impressive Houses of Parliament. Later in the day, ascend Table Mountain via the cable car and marvel at the mesmerizing sunset, treating yourself to a panoramic view of the Mother City. After this unforgettable experience, return to the hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

Destination: Cape Town

Accommodation: The Capetonian

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 3: Cape Peninsula Tour

After breakfast at the hotel, proceed on a private, full-day Cape Point/Peninsula tour. Join us on Africa's premier tour route to the romantic meeting place of the Indian and Atlantic Oceans. The explorer, Sir Francis Drake, called it "the fairest Cape that we saw in the circumference of the globe". This tour offers a full and exciting day out. It includes Hout Bay, a 30-minute cruise to the seal colony (weather permitting), Cape Point Nature Reserve, Cape Point, Cape of Good Hope, swimming in the Indian Ocean is possible, and visiting the penguin colony in Simon's Town. On your return to the hotel, the rest of the evening is at leisure.

Destination: Cape Town

Accommodation: The Capetonian

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 4: Cape Winelands Tour

Today, we embark on a captivating full-day tour to the Cape Winelands, just a short 50-minute drive from Cape Town. The Cape Winelands, nestled in the Western Cape, stand as one of South Africa's major attractions. Here, the allure of flourishing vineyards, elegant Cape Dutch manor homes, and exquisite cuisine, complemented by award-winning wines, creates an enchanting setting amidst valleys embraced by majestic mountains. The charming towns of Stellenbosch and Franschhoek lie within easy reach of Cape Town, boasting a wealth of history and culture as some of the oldest cities in the country. Join us for a guided tour of these two towns, where you'll have the opportunity to visit three top wine estates for delightful wine tastings before we journey back to Cape Town.

Destination: Cape Winelands

Accommodation: The Capetonian

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 5: Cape Town - Aquila Game Reserve - Cape Town

Following breakfast, venture on a Safari Game Drive at Aquila Game Reserve. Aquila offers more than just a remarkable Big 5 safari; it's a sanctuary of luxurious African hospitality conveniently located near Cape Town. Upon arrival, immerse yourself in the Safari experience before indulging in a delectable lunch at the lodge. After the tour, journey back to your hotel in Cape Town for a relaxing overnight stay.

Destination: Balule Nature Reserve (Greater Kruger)

Accommodation: Tremisana Game Lodge

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 6: Cape Town Departure

Breakfast at the Hotel. Check out and transfer to Cape Town Airport for your onward journey.

Destination: Cape Town International Airport

Meal plan: Breakfast & Lunch
