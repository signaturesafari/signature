5 Days Midrange Zanzibar Beach holiday

The ultimate 5 days, 4-night all-inclusive getaway in Zanzibar, starts and ends at the Zanzibar airport. Your retreat unfolds at the enchanting Nungwi on the northern coast, where you'll relax in four nights of unparalleled hospitality. Enjoy private transfers and the amenities of a midrange hotel.

Day 1: Arrival at Zanzibar airport

Upon arrival at Zanzibar airport (ZNZ), you'll receive a warm welcome from a representative of Signature Safari, followed by a brief introduction. Then, you'll be chauffeured directly to your accommodation in the tranquil northern coast of Zanzibar, Nungwi, where you can relax and indulge in an overnight stay.

Destination: Zanzibar (Nungwi)

Accommodation: The Z Hotel

Meal plan: Bed & Breakfast

Day 2-4: Full Days Relaxation in Nungwi (Northern coast of Zanzibar)

Located on the northern tip of Zanzibar, Nungwi is a haven of pristine white beaches and clear turquoise waters, perfect for a romantic honeymoon escape. Whether you prefer unwinding in luxurious hotel accommodations or exploring the vibrant ocean, Nungwi offers both. Dive into thrilling adventures like snorkeling, dolphin tours, and safari blue excursions, arranged either by us or through your hotel with professional guidance. Experience the unique serenity of Nungwi's tidal changes, promising a tranquil and unforgettable four-night stay.

Destination: Zanzibar (Nungwi)

Accommodation: The Z Hotel

Meal plan: Bed & Breakfast drinks

Day 5: Onward Travel to the next destination

After breakfast, lunch or according to your preferred time, you will be picked up by your private driver and take you straight to the airport to catch your flight to the next destination.

Meal plan: Breakfast & Lunch
