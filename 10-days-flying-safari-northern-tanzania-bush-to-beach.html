10 Days Flying Safari Northern Tanzania Bush to Beach

Experience the best of Tanzania with our 10 Days Flying Safari: From Beach to Bush. This unique itinerary offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure, combining the pristine beaches of Zanzibar with the thrilling wildlife encounters of the Serengeti. Enjoy the luxury of flying between destinations, maximizing your time in these iconic locations.

Day 1: Kilimanjaro Airport to Arusha

Upon your arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO), you'll be greeted by your professional guide who will transfer you to your accommodation in Arusha. Nestled at the base of Mount Meru, Arusha is a vibrant city known as the gateway to Tanzania's northern safari circuit. You'll have the rest of the day to relax and recover from your journey.

Accommodation: Lemala Hamerkop

Meal plan: Half Board

Day 2: Flying from Arusha to Kogatende

Enjoy breakfast then depart to the Arusha Airport for your scheduled flight to the Northern Part of Serengeti (Kogatende), upon arrival at the Airport you will be met by our driver guide who will take you in an open vehicle ready for the game drive towards northern part of Mara Region, then after you will be driven for check-in, dinner and overnight at Serengeti Lemala Kuria Hills.

Accommodation: Lemala Kuria Hills

Meal plan: Full Board

Day 3: Game Drive in Northern Serengeti. (Optional Hot air Balloon)

Have an Early breakfast, then depart with your packed lunch and enjoy en-route game drives to the north ern Serengeti National Park where you may witness the Maasai Mara migration as wildebeest cross River. The wildebeest migration can be seen from mid-July through October, depending on rainfall conditions. The Mara River, which flows across a significant portion of the northern Serengeti National Park, presents the migration with its most challenging challenge. It's breath-taking to watch the wildebeest herds rush across the Mara River. Later you will return to your lodge and enjoy dinner and overnight stay at Serengeti Lemala Kuria Hills.

Accommodation: Lemala Kuria Hills

Meal plan: Full Board

Day 4: Northern to Central Serengeti

Today you will wake up for an enroute game drive toward Kogatende airstrip for your flight to the Central part of Serengeti (Seronera) Upon arrival at Seronera you will be met with our driver guide who will pick you up with an open vehicle ready for the game drive in central region of Serengeti National Park, later drive to check in and overnight at Lemala Nanyukie.

Accommodation: Lemala Nanyukie

Meal plan: Full Board

Day 5: Game Drive in Central Serengeti

Enjoy breakfast then depart with packed lunch for a full game drives around central Serengeti central Serengeti contains the largest remaining concentration of plains game in Africa, on a scale, which has no parallel anywhere in the world. The abundance of herbivores includes Wildebeest, Zebra, Gazelle, Topi, Buffalo, and Hartebeest, to name but a few. Serengeti is also famous for Predators, e.g., Lion, Cheetah, Leopard, Hyena and contains a wide variety of birdlife, believed to be in the region of 500 species.  You will enjoy game drives till sunset, then proceed to Serengeti Lemala Nanyukie, for check-in, dinner and overnighti.

Accommodation: Lemala Nanyukie

Meal plan: Full Board

Day 6: Fly Serengeti to Manyara – Ngorongoro

This morning, you'll fly to Manyara Airstrip, where a vehicle will be waiting to transfer you to the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. After checking into your lodge, you'll have the rest of the day at leisure. The Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is one of Africa's most stunning natural wonders, and you'll have time to relax and soak in the breathtaking views from your lodge perched on the crater rim.

Accommodation: Lemala Ngorongoro

Meal plan: Full Board

Day 7: Full Day Ngorongoro crater

You will wake up early morning, having your breakfast in the morning before starting to depart to Ngorongoro Crater for a game drive, the 600 m deep caldera you will descend the crater for a game drive. The Ngorongoro Crater is the world's largest inactive, intact and unfilled volcanic caldera. It has a massive floor of about 260 sq. kms with a depth of over 2000 feet. Ngoro Ngoro Crater is the largest collapsed volcanic crater in the world and fourteen kilometers of isolated natural beauty. Ngorongoro Crater is surrounded by a ring of extinct volcanoes and the floor, dotted with watering holes, shelters some 30,000 animals. The 5-hour game drive on the crater floor will show you a lot of animal action. Keeping the camera ready is definitely recommended. The African elephant, buffalo, Black rhino, Hippos, Hyenas, Cheetahs and Lions are found in plenty. Post the picnic lunch at the beautiful Hippo pool, you will begin a steep ascend to the top exit of the crater. With an incredible experience and loads of memories to cherish, this is the time you bid goodbye to your team. We will then have a picnic lunch at a beautiful picnic site, then we will have game drive before our guide will take us to Ngorongoro the Lemala for relaxation, dinner and overnight..

Accommodation: Lemala Ngorongoro

Meal plan: Full Board

Day 8: Flight to Tarangire National Park

After breakfast, you will be transferred back to Manyara Airstrip for a short flight to Tarangire National Park. Upon arrival, embark on an initial game drive en route to your lodge, spotting the park's famous large elephant herds and ancient baobab trees. After checking in and having lunch, you'll have an afternoon game drive to explore more of Tarangire’ s unique landscape and rich wildlife.

Accommodation: Lemala Mpingo ridge

Meal plan: Full Board

Day 9: Full Day in Tarangire National Park

Today is dedicated to a full day of game viewing in Tarangire National Park. Known for its high density of elephants and iconic baobab trees, the park offers excellent wildlife viewing opportunities. In addition to elephants, you may see lions, leopards, cheetahs, and a variety of antelopes. The Tarangire River, which runs through the park, attracts wildlife year-round, making it a perfect spot for game drives. Enjoy a packed lunch in the park to maximize your time in the field.

Accommodation: Lemala Mpingo ridge

Meal plan: Full Board

Day 10: Departure and Return to Zanzibar

On your final day, you’ll have a morning game drive in Tarangire before heading to the airstrip for your flight back to Manyara for your flight to Zanzibar. This marks the end of your incredible Tanzanian safari, filled with unforgettable wildlife encounters and breathtaking landscapes.

Meal plan: Breakfast, Picnic Lunch box
