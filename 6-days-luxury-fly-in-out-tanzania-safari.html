Day 2: Arusha to Lake Manyara airstrip to Ngorongoro crater

Today, you'll depart early after enjoying a delicious breakfast at around 6:30 AM, heading to Arusha Airport to catch your 8:00 AM flight. You will arrive at Manyara Airstrip at 8:25 AM, where your private driver guide will pick you up and drive you to the Ngorongoro Crater. This breathtaking setting is the best place in East Africa to see the Big Five and is renowned as one of the world's most stunning natural wildlife safari sites. It also offers a unique opportunity to interact with the Maasai tribe.
As you reach the crater's edge, the 20 km diameter caldera unfolds before you in all its stunning glory. You'll descend into the crater on an adventurous trail, exploring this World Heritage Site, which is home to over 25,000 animals, including a wide range of bird species, hyenas, and the endangered black rhino. A hot lunch will be served at a picnic spot by the hippo pool, after which you'll continue exploring the crater.
Following your exploration, you'll ascend the crater and drive straight to your accommodation for dinner and an overnight stay.

Day 3: Ngorongoro to Serengeti National Park

Early in the morning, after breakfast at around 8:00 AM or at your preferred time, you will head straight to Serengeti National Park. Known for its vast plains and incredible wildlife concentrations, Serengeti is the most famous park on the northern circuit, particularly renowned for the Great Migration.
You will arrive in Serengeti by midday, where you will begin exploring the park. Lunch will be served at a picnic site around 12:30 PM. Afterward, you will continue your unlimited game drive until the evening, when you will proceed to your tented camp or lodge for dinner and an overnight stay.

Destination: Serengeti National Park

Accommodation: Kubu Kubu Tented Lodge

Meal plan: All-inclusive meals (B, L & D) + drinks

Day 4-5: Full days game drive inside Serengeti National Park

Early in the morning, after breakfast at around 8:00 AM or at your preferred time, you will embark on a full-day game drive exploring the vast plains of Serengeti National Park. This park is home to a diverse array of wildlife, including the Big Five: lion, elephant, buffalo, rhino, and leopard. A hot lunch will be served inside the park around 12:30 PM, accompanied by soft drinks and unlimited drinking water.
You will continue your unlimited game drive until the evening, then head to your tented camp or lodge for dinner and an overnight stay. You will spend a total of three nights within Serengeti National Park.

Destination: Serengeti National Park

Accommodation: Kubu Kubu Tented Lodge

Meal plan: All-inclusive meals (B, L & D) + drinks

Day 6: Seronera airstrip to Arusha

Early morning after breakfast, you will drive straight from your accommodation inside Serengeti to the airstrip at the central Serengeti to catch your onwards flight to Arusha.

Meal plan: Breakfast & Lunch
