3 Days Mikumi National Park from Zanzibar

Embark on a captivating 3 days excursion to Mikumi National Park and delve into the natural marvels of Tanzania. Prepare to be awestruck by the majestic wildlife and immerse yourself in the rich cultural tapestry with a visit to a Maasai Boma. Here's what to anticipate: Thrilling day safari through Mikumi National Park, encountering a myriad of wildlife and bird species. Cultural immersion with a visit to a Maasai village, offering a unique and enriching experience. Experience the exhilaration of a nocturnal game drive in Mikumi, where the hidden treasures of the wilderness reveal themselves under the shimmering starlight.

Day 1: Let's begin the Adventure- from Zanzibar to the Mikumi- Wild Heart of Africa

Embark on your African safari journey commencing from Zanzibar. Opt for a breathtaking aerial view of Africa by choosing to fly to Mikumi (additional fee applies), or opt for an equally captivating journey via ferry, immersing yourself in the 1.5-hour crossing to the continent. From there, a 5–6 hour drive through the mesmerizing African landscape awaits, captivating your senses with its untamed beauty. En route, a lunch break will provide the perfect opportunity to recharge for the remainder of your adventure. Welcome to Mikumi! Upon your arrival, we extend an invitation to explore a Maasai village—a preview of the authentic experiences awaiting you. Immerse yourself in the rich culture and traditions of the Maasai people, creating memories that will last a lifetime. Optional for an Additional Fee - Night Game Drive: For those craving nocturnal adventures, indulge in an optional night safari, offering a unique perspective on African wildlife under the veil of darkness.

Destination: Mikumi National Park

Accommodation: Camp Bastian

Meal plan: Lunch & Dinner

Day 2: Full Day at Mikumi

After breakfast, continue with your game drive in Mikumi. Mid-morning and afternoon program: Embark on an exciting game drive within Mikumi National Park, renowned for its diverse wildlife. You can spot lions, elephants, giraffes, and various antelope species, among other animals. Enjoy a packed lunch in the park's scenic surroundings, depending on your safari package.

Destination: Mikumi National Park.

Accommodation: Camp Bastian.

Meals: Full Board (B, L & D).

Day 3: Return to Zanzibar

Post breakfast return to Dar Es Salaam for ferry to Zanzibar. If an individual opts for the flight option(for an additional surcharge), they will be transferred to the Mikumi airstrip after breakfast for a flight to Zanzibar (ZNZ). Upon arrival at Zanzibar Airport or the ferry terminal, a transfer will be arranged to the hotel.
