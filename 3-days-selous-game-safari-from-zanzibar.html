3 Days Selous Game Reserve Safari from Zanzibar

Embark on our renowned 3 days Selous Game Reserve Safari, a cherished short safari excursion from Zanzibar Island. This journey stands out as one of the premier options for short tours departing from Zanzibar, offering a rare opportunity to explore the Selous Game Reserve. Our itinerary allows for a seamless transition from Zanzibar to Dar es Salaam International Airport, followed by a scenic road trip from Dar es Salaam to the reserve. While we cover flights from Zanzibar to Dar es Salaam, please note that hotel transfers within Zanzibar are not included. However, should you require airport transfers in Zanzibar, simply provide us with your hotel details for a hassle-free pick-up and drop-off service.

Day 1: Fly Zanzibar to Dar es Salaam - Selous

This morning, board a flight from Zanzibar to Dar es Salaam, where your safari guide eagerly awaits your arrival. Touching down in Dar es Salaam around 08:00 hrs, your driver will swiftly escort you to the Selous Game Reserve, reaching your destination by lunchtime. After settling into your lodge or camp and indulging in a hearty lunch, take some time to unwind. In the afternoon, embark on a thrilling boat safari from the nearby jetty, an enchanting voyage along the Great Rufiji. Witness the majestic presence of crocodiles, hippos, and the playful black and white colobus monkeys. Marvel at the diverse avian life, including fish eagles and pied kingfishers, as they grace the riverbanks. Keep your eyes peeled for the mesmerizing wildlife encounters as they converge near the water's edge. As the day draws to a close, soak in the breathtaking sunset vistas before returning to your lodge or camp for a delectable dinner and a restful overnight stay. Your accommodations, either Selous Tented Camp or Selous Mapumziko Lodge, perch serenely on the banks of the Rufiji River, promising an immersive wilderness experience.

Destination: Selous Game Reserve

Meal plan: Lunch & Dinner

Day 2: Full Day at Selous Game Reserve

Begin your day with an early morning breakfast at your camp or lodge, preparing for an exhilarating adventure ahead. After breakfast, head to the gates of the Selous Game Reserve for a full day of immersive game viewing in Africa's largest game reserve. Throughout the day, traverse the expansive reserve, embarking on wildlife game drives in pursuit of the diverse array of animals and birds that call Selous home. Keep your eyes peeled for sightings of graceful gazelles, towering giraffes, majestic elephants, and the iconic wildebeests and zebras. With a stroke of luck, you may even catch a glimpse of the elusive African wild dogs, among the rarest of sightings. Named in honor of the esteemed explorer Frederick Selous, the reserve holds UNESCO World Heritage status, a testament to its ecological significance. Spanning an area roughly equivalent to Switzerland, Selous encompasses untamed African wilderness, characterized by a captivating landscape featuring savannah woodlands, rolling plains, and ancient rocky outcrops sculpted by the Rufiji River over time. As night falls, retreat to the comfort of Selous Tented Camp or Selous Mapumziko Lodge, where all meals are included, ensuring a restful overnight stay immersed in the heart of nature's grandeur.

Destination: Selous Game Reserve.

Meal plan: Lunch & Dinner.

Day 3: Walking Safari in Selous - Fly Dar Es Salaam to Zanzibar.

Start your day with an early morning breakfast before embarking on an unforgettable walking safari within the Selous Game Reserve. This rare opportunity allows you to intimately experience the African wilderness on foot, drawing you closer to its captivating wildlife. Your safety is paramount, so you'll be accompanied by an armed ranger, doubling as a knowledgeable naturalist guide. Following the walking safari, begin your journey back to Dar es Salaam by road. If you opt for the road trip, anticipate arriving in Dar es Salaam by late afternoon. From there, you'll be transported to Dar es Salaam International Airport for your return flight to Zanzibar Island, concluding your remarkable safari adventure.
