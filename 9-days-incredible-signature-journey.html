9 Days Incredible Signature Journey

Indulge in the ultimate safari experience, granting you unparalleled access to Botswana's wilderness and its top attractions. This opulent journey promises exceptional game sightings while guiding you through three of Botswana's most captivating highlights. Extend your adventure with a visit to the majestic Victoria Falls, seamlessly integrated into this 8-night itinerary. Immerse yourself in lavish accommodations set amidst pristine landscapes, epitomizing the essence of Botswana's hospitality. Embark on a variety of thrilling activities including game drives, boat excursions, canoeing, walking safaris, fishing, and beyond, ensuring an unforgettable exploration of Africa's untamed beauty.

Day 1-2: A Private Okavango Retreat

Commencing from Maun Airport in Botswana, your journey embarks with a private charter flight to the northern reaches of the Okavango Delta, where the enchanting Xugana Island Lodge awaits. Nestled beneath towering trees on a secluded island, Xugana epitomizes the essence of a water-based camp. Here, guests are immersed in the serene beauty of the delta, exploring its labyrinthine waterways by boat, canoe, and foot. Each day commences with a tranquil mokoro (canoe) excursion, expertly guided to unveil the myriad birdlife and amphibian wonders adorning the banks of crystal-clear rivers. Island-hopping escapades and guided walking safaris unveil the delta's rich tapestry of wildlife, while evenings are graced with starlit suppers and convivial fireside gatherings at camp.

Destination: Okavango Delta

Accommodation: Xugana Island Lodge

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 3-5: A Wild Corner of Moremi Game Reserve

A brief flight from Xugana Island Lodge transports you to Camp Moremi, an enchanting land-based retreat nestled alongside a lagoon teeming with hippos, marking the boundary of the delta. Here, the focal point shifts to exhilarating game drives, and rightfully so. Moremi Game Reserve's untamed expanse boasts a storied reputation for unparalleled wildlife sightings. Elephants grace the landscape alongside a plethora of antelope species, buffalo, giraffes, and zebras. Amidst this thriving ecosystem, predators reign supreme, with big cats and wild dogs calling this territory home. Elevated tented suites at Camp Moremi offer panoramic views of the lagoon, ensconced beneath the canopy of riverside trees alive with avian melodies, encapsulating the essence of safari enchantment.

Destination: Moremi Game Reserve

Accommodation: Camp Moremi

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 6-8: Discover the Savute Wildlife Magic

Embark on another short flight to kickstart your day. Departing from Moremi, you'll descend into the heart of Chobe National Park, landing in the picturesque Savute region. Here, indulge in a two-night stay at the lavish Savute Safari Lodge, boasting opulent chalets with panoramic views of the vibrant Savute Channel. Witness the majestic spectacle of elephants and antelopes frolicking in the waters below, while the true excitement unfolds during Savute's exhilarating game drives. Accompanied by seasoned guides, immerse yourself in the quest for the park's iconic inhabitants: elephants, lions, and spotted hyenas. Amidst Savute's rugged terrain, leopards stealthily roam the rocky hills, cheetahs keenly observe impala herds, and during the summer months, witness the awe-inspiring zebra migration that paints the landscape with vibrant hues.

Destination: Chobe National Park

Accommodation: Savute Safari Lodge

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 9: The End of your Journey

Following breakfast, relish in the final morning adventure. Later, you'll be chauffeured by road to Maun Airport for your departure flight. Should you wish to prolong your visit in Africa's adventure hub, Victoria Falls, or include an idyllic Indian Ocean beach getaway or a journey to Cape Town, simply let us know, and we'll make the arrangements for you.

Destination: Maun City

Meal plan: Breakfast & Lunch
