4 Days Okavango delta Boteti River Tented Safari

Description

4 Days Okavango delta Boteti River Tented Safari

Indulge in a visual banquet and revel in the majestic wonders of wildlife and nature! Embark on a four-day journey of unforgettable experiences, anchored along the picturesque banks of the Boteti River, with adventures spanning the Okavango Delta and Moremi Game Reserve. Your sanctuary for this expedition rests upon the tranquil banks of the Boteti River, a tributary sourced from the heart of the Okavango Delta.

Day 1: Arrival and Sunset Mokoro Safari

Upon your arrival at Maun Airport, you'll be greeted by your ranger and guide for a scenic 20-minute drive to your tented lodge nestled along the tranquil banks of the Boteti River. After a swift check-in process at the lodge, depending on your arrival time, you'll have the opportunity to indulge in a quick lunch or take a refreshing dip in the pool, all while immersing yourself in the natural beauty of the surroundings. Following this, embark on an enchanting 2-hour sunset Mokoro safari along the Boteti River, where you'll have the chance to encounter hippos and crocodiles at the river's edge, all while marveling at the breathtaking birdlife. Guided by experienced mokoro guides, you'll gain insights into the river's ecosystem and its diverse wildlife. A stop at one of the riverbanks offers a moment to savor a small refreshment while basking in the splendor of the setting sun. After your memorable mokoro experience, take some time to freshen up before joining us around the open fire for an evening of camaraderie. Your overnight accommodation awaits in a cozy safari tented room, ensuring a restful night amidst the wilderness.

Destination: Moremi Game Reserve

Accommodation: Boteti Tented Safari Lodge

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 2: Full Day Mokoro Safari – Okavango Delta

As the sun gently rises, awakening the wilderness around you, breathe in the crisp morning air, invigorating your senses. Delight awaits at the main deck area, where a steaming cup of coffee or tea paired with homemade biscuits sets the perfect tone for the day ahead. With a delectable packed breakfast in hand, prepare for an early start to your full-day mokoro safari in the captivating Okavango Delta. Embark on your mokoro safari, navigating through the intricate reed channels of the Delta under the guidance of your expert guide. Along the way, immerse yourself in unique interpretations of nature and discover the traditional and medicinal uses of wild plants within this vibrant ecosystem. Pause for a rejuvenating break on one of the Delta's verdant islands, where you'll savor your packed lunch before embarking on a captivating nature walk, unveiling the secrets of this rich and diverse habitat. Following a moment of relaxation, continue your exploration of the Delta's boundless channels, with the freedom to halt at any intriguing island that captures your curiosity. Upon returning to the lodge, take some time to refresh before indulging in a sumptuous dinner served under the twinkling stars in the open boma, an enchanting conclusion to a day filled with adventure and discovery.

Destination: Moremi Game Reserve

Accommodation: Boteti Tented Safari Lodge

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 3: Full Day Game Drive in Moremi / Khwai Reserves

Awaken just as the sun begins its gentle ascent, filling your lungs with the invigorating morning air. Make your way to the main deck area, where a freshly brewed cup of coffee or tea and delectable homemade biscuits await, setting the perfect start to your day. With a delicious packed breakfast in hand, prepare for an early departure on your full-day safari game drive in Moremi or Khwai Reserves. Embark on this exhilarating journey aboard an open game vehicle specially designed to enhance your wildlife experience, allowing you to immerse yourself fully in sightings with all your senses. Your seasoned game ranger will provide insightful commentary, unveiling the nuances of nature and interpreting wildlife behavior in a captivating manner. Savor a stylishly served packed lunch at one of the picturesque rest camps along your route, replenishing your energy for the adventures ahead. By approximately 4:00 pm, return to the lodge, where you'll have ample time to freshen up and bask in the breathtaking sunset views overlooking the Boteti River from our deck. As night falls, gather under the star-studded sky in the open boma for a delightful dinner, culminating your day of exploration with a touch of wilderness elegance.

Destination: Moremi Game Reserve

Accommodation: Boteti Tented Safari Lodge

Meal plan: Breakfast & lunch

Day 4: Morning Relaxation and Departure

Indulge in a leisurely and delectable breakfast this morning, allowing yourself to soak in the stunning surroundings one final time before bidding farewell. As you prepare for checkout and transfer to Maun, the warm and welcoming lodge staff will extend their heartfelt farewells. Choose between a transfer back to Maun Airport or a drop-off point of your preference within Maun. With this, your memorable tour comes to a close, leaving you with cherished memories of your unforgettable journey.

Meal plan: Breakfast & lunch
