Day 1: Nairobi - Masai Mara National Reserve

This safari is perfect for those traveling with infants and elders, providing mid-range luxury accommodations that are easily adaptable. It's also ideal for novice photographers, featuring extended stays, diverse wildlife experiences, and breathtaking landscapes, beginning in Masai Mara.

Destination: Masai Mara National Reserve

Accommodation: & Beyond Kichwa Tembo Tented Camp

Meal plan: All-inclusive

Day 2: Masai Mara National Reserve

After enjoying breakfast at the camp, you'll embark on a morning game drive in the reserve. Lunch boxes will be provided as you anticipate spending the entire day out. You have the option to continue game driving until sunset or participate in an optional hot air balloon ride. Additionally, you can visit a Maasai Village in the afternoon. During the game drive, you'll have the opportunity to spot a variety of wildlife, including lions, cheetahs, elephants, wildebeests, and gazelles. The reserve is also home to more than 300 species of birds. After a full day of exploring, you'll return to the camp for dinner and your overnight stay.

Destination: Masai Mara National Reserve.

Accommodation: & Beyond Kichwa Tembo Tented Camp.

Meal plan: All-inclusive.

Day 3: Masai Mara National Reserve - Nairobi

Following a sunrise drive in the morning, you'll have breakfast before heading to the airport for your flight back to Nairobi. In Nairobi, you'll enjoy lunch, engage in city sightseeing, and later be transported to the airport for your return flight home.

Destination: Masai Mara National Reserve.

Meals: Breakfast & Lunch.
