5 Days Lodge Kruger Safari

Description

5 Days Lodge Kruger Safari

Experience the pinnacle of our tour with a captivating city exploration of Cape Town, renowned as one of the world's most stunning cities. Venture on a mesmerizing Cape Peninsula tour and indulge in a full-day adventure through the exquisite Cape Winelands, where you'll savor some of the finest wines globally. Conclude your journey with a memorable overnight stay at Aquila Game Reserve, complete with both sunrise and sunset safaris for an immersive wildlife encounter.

Day 1: Transfer, Sunset Drive and Boma Braai

Begin your adventure with a pick-up from your hotel in JHB or O.R. Tambo Airport, setting off through the picturesque Highveld grasslands towards Tremisana Game Lodge in Kruger Park. Upon arrival, hop aboard an open Landcruiser for a captivating 4-hour Sunset Drive, your inaugural opportunity to immerse yourself in Africa's rich fauna and flora. Witness the breathtaking African sunset and, as darkness falls, use a spotlight to observe nocturnal animals. Later, delight in a BUSH BBQ dinner at the Marula Tree Boma. Afterward, continue your exploration with a Night Drive before retiring for the night in comfortable en suite air-conditioned chalets.

Destination: Balule Nature Reserve (Greater Kruger)

Accommodation: Tremisana Game Lodge

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 2: Bush walk and sundowner game drive

As the sun rises, embark on a journey to the majestic Olifants River for a thrilling 4-hour Bush Walk. Accompanied by knowledgeable rangers, traverse through the bush at a pace tailored to your comfort, immersing yourself in the exhilarating experience of tracking big animals. The rush of adrenaline will leave a lasting impression, knowing your rangers are equipped for safety. Return to Tremisana for a hearty brunch. The remainder of the day offers leisurely pursuits, whether it's relaxing in your air-conditioned chalet, taking a refreshing swim, or delving into wildlife magazines in our Lapa Lounge. Lunch will be served in the Lapa at 13:30. At 15:30, embark on a 4-hour sundowner game drive, during which our rangers maintain constant communication to track the movements of the animals. Return to Tremisana at 19:30 after a memorable wildlife adventure.

Destination: Balule Nature Reserve (Greater Kruger)

Accommodation: Tremisana Game Lodge

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 3: Full Day Kruger National Park

Embark on a journey to the Orpen Gate of Kruger aboard our custom-built open-sided Game Viewer, offering unobstructed views of the wildlife and allowing you to immerse yourself in the sounds and scents of the bush without the barrier of glass windows. Along the way, enjoy regular stops at picnic spots, rest camps, and points of interest. The Orpen-Satara-Nwanedzi region is known for its expansive plains, drawing in large herds of zebra, giraffe, and antelope, which, in turn, attract numerous predators. In fact, this area boasts the highest concentration of lions in Africa.

Destination: Balule Nature Reserve (Greater Kruger)

Accommodation: Tremisana Game Lodge

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 4: Kruger National Park

Embark on another full-day game drive in the Kruger National Park, traveling to the Orpen Gate aboard our custom-built open-sided game viewer. Experience wildlife viewing without the barrier of glass windows, allowing you to fully immerse yourself in the sights, sounds, and scents of the bush. Throughout the journey, enjoy regular stops at picnic spots, rest camps, and points of interest. Conclude the day with dinner and an overnight stay at Tremisana Game Lodge.

Destination: Balule Nature Reserve (Greater Kruger)

Accommodation: Tremisana Game Lodge

Meal plan: Breakfast, lunch & Dinner

Day 5: Sunrise Drive and Three Rondawels

Experience the beauty of a Sunrise Drive before breakfast. Following this, embark on a scenic journey via the Panorama Route, where you'll encounter breathtaking sights such as the Three Rondawels and the majestic Blyde River Canyon. We'll return to Johannesburg in the late afternoon, with airport drop-off available upon arrangement.
