Discover Rwanda’s Iconic Wilderness

Rwanda is famed for its mountain gorilla trekking, a once-in-a-lifetime experience that brings you face-to-face with one of the world’s most endangered species. As part of the Signature Safari itineraries under the Rwanda Safari Packages, guests are led through the magical pathways of Volcanoes National Park. This region is home to bamboo forests, misty highlands, and abundant wildlife that thrive in one of Africa’s most biodiverse landscapes.

Beyond gorillas, these packages also include visits to Nyungwe Forest National Park, where canopy walks reveal hidden bird species and primates swinging gracefully overhead. Akagera National Park offers a contrasting scene — golden savannas, shimmering lakes, and the Big Five in a peaceful and less-crowded environment. With such diverse landscapes and wildlife, the Rwanda Safari Packages provide a rich mix of adventure, photography, and cultural immersion.
